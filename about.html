Professional Summary

I am Dinesh Kumar, a Chartered Accountant with over 37 years of experience in the fields of accounting, auditing, insurance surveys, regulatory investigations, and financial compliance. I have served in key roles such as Assistant Provident Fund Commissioner and Inspecting Officer with the Department of Company Affairs, and have also worked with reputed institutions like Mahindra & Mahindra, Dalmia Cement, and ICICI Bank.

My core expertise lies in fraud detection, risk assessment, Bankers Indemnity Policy consultancy, and insurance claim advisory — especially for banks and financial institutions. I’ve conducted numerous insurance surveys, prepared banking fraud reports, and advised insurance brokers to help improve policy clarity and streamline claims.

I combine forensic precision, regulatory insight, and practical advisory to offer strategic consultancy services to corporates, banks, and insurance intermediaries with a strong emphasis on confidentiality, integrity, and timely execution.

I am also registered as an Independent Director in the official MCA Data Bank, enabling me to contribute to corporate boards in a governance, compliance, and oversight capacity.
